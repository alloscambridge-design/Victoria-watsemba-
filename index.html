Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Cambridge curriculum internationally recognized?

Yes. Cambridge qualifications like IGCSE and A-Levels are accepted by universities worldwide, including Harvard, Oxford, and University of Cape Town.

What equipment does my child need?

Just a laptop, tablet, or smartphone with stable internet. We use Zoom and Google Classroom for live lessons.

Can students join mid-term?

Yes. We offer rolling admissions with a catch-up plan and access to recordings.

How are exams handled?

We help you register for Cambridge exams at authorized centers in your country and provide full exam prep.

What's the class size?

Group classes have 8-12 students max. 1-to-1 tutoring is also available.
